MOVIE REPORT – TRON: LEGACY

1. TITLE (Include genre, director, nationality)

Title: Tron: Legacy

Genre: Science fiction / Action

Director: Joseph Kosinski

Nationality: American film

2. SUMMARY – What is it about?

Tron: Legacy is about Sam Flynn, a young man who enters a digital world inside a computer system. There he meets programs that look like people, discovers that his father has been trapped for years, and must fight against a powerful program called Clu to escape the Grid and save both worlds.

3. GIVE YOUR OWN OPINION – Would you recommend it? Why/why not?

I would definitely recommend this movie because the visual effects and music are amazing. The idea of what would happen if we could enter a digital world is very interesting and original. Also, the story is exciting and emotional.

4. WHAT IS THE MESSAGE OF THE MOVIE?

The movie teaches that:

  • technology can be powerful, but it needs human values
  • perfection is impossible
  • we should appreciate people, not just systems

It also explores the importance of family and facing your past.

5. WRITE 5 QUESTIONS ABOUT THE STORY OR THE MESSAGE

  1. What would you do if you could enter a digital world like the Grid?
  2. Why did Clu become the main threat in the system?
  3. What does the movie say about the dangers of perfection?
  4. How does Sam change after meeting his father in the Grid?
  5. What role does technology play in our lives today compared to the movie?
